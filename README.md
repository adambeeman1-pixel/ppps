# Polaris Parenting Plan Preparation System (PPPS)

A guided client preparation system built by the Polaris Parenting Project.

## What This Is

The PPPS is a structured, interactive tool that helps parents understand how parenting plans work before they engage with attorneys, mediators, or the other parent. It is not legal advice and does not generate a parenting plan. It generates a preparation document — the artifact a client brings to their first professional meeting.

## Structure

13 sections organized across four layers:

**Orientation**
- Introduction
- How Parenting Plans Are Developed

**Core Parenting Plan**
- Physical Custody
- Holidays & Special Days
- Legal Custody
- Education / Medical / Activities
- Transportation & Exchanges
- Communication Provisions
- Common Drafting Pitfalls
- Modification & Dispute Resolution
- Negotiation Reality

**Additional Considerations**
- Travel, passports, right of first refusal, new relationships, extracurricular costs, technology

**Closing**
- Closing Reflection

## Technical

- React 18
- Local storage for session persistence
- No external API calls
- Deployed via Vercel

## Legal

This system is for educational and preparation purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice and does not replace an attorney, mediator, or other legal professional.
